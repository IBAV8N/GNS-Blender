###### King Air C90B EVO ######

Authors: pilotmarcog, IBAV8N, Jeffory, FSPilot_CZ
Usage: Free for personal use

### INSTALLATION/UPDATE GUIDE ###

This is a complete plane. Optional liveries are downloaded separately and expanded into the liveries folder under the plane's
main folder. Otherwise you are good to go as-is.

Plane requires AviTab plugin installed. 

GTN version requires the RealityXP GTN plugin also.
